    Story of The Spintex Plaza Facilities

    Spintex Plaza is owned and managed by Textile Trico Manufacturing Company Limited which belongs to a group of companies that includes Interface Limited and Nsawam Foam Company Limited. This group of companies started operations in 1967 and is family owned.The vision of the owners is for Spintex Plaza to be the most sought after residential and commercial accommodation in the Spintex Road enclave resulting in an achievable 100% occupancy rate.
